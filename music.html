Shubh Varshney's Favorite Music

Who doesn't love music! When I'm doing something and if it seems really quiet or boring, I go to music! However, I often do not pay attention to who I am listening to or what I am listening to. But I do remember some singers and songs that really stood out to me!

Song 1: Evidemment by La Zarra

I really love this song. I know a bit of French, so this song sounds really beautiful and fulfilling.

Song 2: Tu Maan Meri Jaan by Nick Jonas and King

This song is a really good mash up between two languages. I know both Hindi and English, and I love how both the singers bring the song together!

Song 3: Never Gonna Give You Up by Rick Astley

This song is pretty infamous, but I think I've heard it so much it's pretty good. However, it's funny when someone "rickrolls" someone with a song such as this one.

Other songs I like.
